Understanding Usergroups and Permissions

Back to Top
A usergroup is a means of grouping users (registered members of a forum) together, all of whom have the same permissions, or access rights.

Concepts important in vBulletin usergroups and permissions include the following:
  • Channels
  • Group membership
  • Inheritance
  • Join requests
  • Permissions
  • Promotion
  • Users and product functionality available in the Users menu
  • Usergroups and product functionality available in the Usergroups menu
